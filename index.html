Containment Inc.

A Simulation of Hope and Strategy

Year 2050. A mysterious and highly contagious pathogen emerges from the thawing permafrost in Siberia. This virus, reminiscent of the Black Death, spreads rapidly. Panic ensues, and the world turns to Dr. Aria Chen, a renowned epidemiologist, to lead the charge against this crisis.

Your mission: Contain the outbreak, develop a vaccine, and manage the social and political upheaval that threatens humanity's survival.
